SALLIE MAE  SLM OFFERS FIXED RATE NOTES WASHINGTON MARCH 13 THE STUDENT LOAN MARKETING ASSOCIATION SALLIE MAE ANNOUNCED AN OFFERING OF 100 MLN DLRS OF THREE YEAR FIXED RATE NOTES REDEEMABLE AT MATURITY IN THE US DOLLAR EQUIVALENT OF 1452 MLN AUSTRALIAN DLRS IT SAID THE 12125 PCT OBLIGATION WILL BE PRICED AT PAR AND SOLD IN THE UNITED STATES IN A PUBLIC OFFERING MANAGED BY MORGAN STANLEY INTEREST PAYMENTS ON THE NOTES WILL BE MADE SEMIANNUALLY IN US DLRS STARTING ON SEPT 20 1987 SALLIE MAE SAID IT ADDED THAT THE NOTES ARE DUE MARCH 20 1990 SALLIE MAE SAID IT WILL ENTER INTO A SEPARATE CURRENCY EXCHANGE AGREEMENT TO CONVERT THE PRINCIPAL PAYMENT AT MATURITY FROM AUSTRALIAN DOLLARS TO US DOLLARS IT SAID THE NOTES WILL BE ISSUED IN REGISTERED FORM AND BE AVAILABLE IN DENOMINATIONS OF 10000 US DLRS AND IN 5000 DLR INCREMENTS ABOVE THAT AMOUNT SALLIE MAE SAID PROCEEDS WILL BE USED FOR GENERAL CORPORATE PURCHASES
