CANADA JANUARY TRADE SURPLUS 533 MLN DLRS OTTAWA MAR 12 CANADA HAD A TRADE SURPLUS OF 533 MLN DLRS IN JANUARY COMPARED WITH AN UPWARD REVISED 965 MLN DLRS SURPLUS IN DECEMBER STATISTICS CANADA SAID THE DECEMBER SURPLUS ORIGINALLY WAS REPORTED AT 964 MLN DLRS THE JANUARY SURPLUS LAST YEAR WAS 119 BILLION DLRS JANUARY EXPORTS SEASONALLY ADJUSTED WERE 972 BILLION DLRS AGAINST 1039 BILLION IN DECEMBER AND 1089 BILLION IN JANUARY 1986 JANUARY IMPORTS WERE 919 BILLION DLRS AGAINST 943 BILLION IN DECEMBER AND 971 BILLION IN JANUARY 1986
