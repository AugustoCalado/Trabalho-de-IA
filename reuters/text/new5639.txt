CCC ACCEPTS BONUS BID ON BARLEY TO CYRPUS WASHINGTON MARCH 16 THE COMMODITY CREDIT CORPORATION CCC HAS ACCEPTED A BID ON AN EXPORT BONUS TO COVER THE SALE OF 8000 TONNES OF BARLEY TO CYPRUS THE US AGRICULTURE DEPARTMENT SAID THE BARLEY IS FOR SHIPMENT MAY 5 20 AND THE BONUS OF 3932 DLRS PER TONNE WAS MADE TO HARVEST STATES COOPERATIVES THE DEPARTMENT SAID THE BONUS WILL BE PAID IN THE FORM OF COMMODITIES FROM THE INVENTORY OF CCC STOCKS THE DEPARTMENT SAID AN ADDITIONAL 42000 TONNES OF US BARLEY ARE STILL AVAILABLE TO CYPRUS UNDER THE EXPORT ENHANCEMENT PROGRAM INITIATIVE ANNOUNCED ON AUGUST 26 1986
