NEW ZEALAND GOVERNMENT BORROWS 100 MLN STERLING  WELLINGTON MARCH 16 THE NEW ZEALAND GOVERNMENT BORROWED 100 MLN STG ON THE EUROSTERLING MARKET TO REFINANCE A LOAN OF THE SAME AMOUNT MATURING IN JUNE FINANCE MINISTER ROGER DOUGLAS SAID IN A STATEMENT THE COUPON RATE IS 962 PCT AND THE LOAN LIFE IS EIGHT YEARS DOUGLAS SAID THE COUPON RATE IS THE LOWEST OBTAINED BY THE GOVERNMENT ON A STERLING LOAN SINCE 1971
