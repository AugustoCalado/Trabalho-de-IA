CCC ACCEPTS BONUS BID ON SEMOLINA TO EGYPT WASHINGTON MARCH 5 THE COMMODITY CREDIT CORPORATION CCC HAS ACCEPTED A BID FOR AN EXPORT BONUS TO COVER A SALE OF 2000 TONNES OF SEMOLINA TO EGYPT THE US AGRICULTURE DEPARTMENT SAID THE DEPARTMENT SAID THE BONUS WAS 23391 DLRS PER TONNE AND WAS MADE TO INTERNATIONAL MULTIFOODS CORP THE BONUS WILL BE PAID IN THE FORM OF COMMODITIES FROM CCC STOCKS THE SEMOLINA IS FOR SHIPMENT DURING JUNE 1987 IT SAID AN ADDITIONAL 13000 TONNES OF SEMOLINA ARE STILL AVAILABLE TO EGYPT UNDER THE EXPORT ENHANCEMENT PROGRAM INITIATIVE ANNOUNCED ON AUGUST 6 1986 IT SAID
